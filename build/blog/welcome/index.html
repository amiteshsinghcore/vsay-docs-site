Welcome to VSAY Terminal

February 6, 2026 · 3 min read
Amitesh Singh
Software Engineer
Yesveer Singh
Software Engineer

We are thrilled to introduce VSAY Terminal — a full-fledged Privileged Access Management (PAM) and Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) solution.

Our Story​

VSAY Terminal started as one of a idea of Amitesh that why now we make the thing simple and secure in today's world where everything is simply accessible — Then we formed a team with Yesveer and Amitesh where the vision was to simplify how teams manage privileged access to their servers. What began as an idea during countless hours of juggling SSH keys, remembering IP addresses, and managing team permissions, eventually became a mission. Then, together they designed and developed what VSAY Terminal is today. The two engineers shared a common frustration: managing SSH connections and privileged access across multiple servers shouldn't be this complicated. There had to be a better way.

And so, VSAY Terminal was born — a secure, intuitive, and powerful PAM platform that makes privileged access management effortless for teams of all sizes.

Special Thanks to Yesveer Singh who has given his whole effort to make this dream come true.

What Makes Us Different?​

Unlike traditional PAM solutions, VSAY Terminal is built with developer experience at its core. We don't just provide a web portal — we bring privileged access management directly into your workflow:

Multi-Channel Accessibility​

Access MethodDescription
Web TerminalBrowser-based SSH access with full terminal capabilities
VSAY VSCode ExtensionManage and connect to servers directly from your IDE
VSAY Shell CLICommand-line tool for automation and scripting
Agent-Based AccessDeploy agents for seamless, secure connections

Enterprise-Grade Security​

  • External Authentication — Integrate with your existing identity provider using Keycloak for OIDC/SSO
  • MTLS (Mutual TLS) — Certificate-based authentication for maximum security
  • Complete Audit Trail — Every session, every command, fully logged and traceable

Full-Fledged PAM & RBAC Solution​

VSAY Terminal provides everything you need for privileged access management:

Privileged Access Management (PAM)​

  • Centralized control over all SSH connections
  • Session recording and playback
  • Just-in-time access provisioning
  • Credential vaulting and rotation

Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)​

  • Granular permission management
  • Custom role definitions
  • Team and organization hierarchy
  • Machine-level access policies

What We Built​

After months of designing, coding, and refining, VSAY Terminal is ready to transform how you manage privileged access:

  • Secure SSH Access — Enterprise-grade encryption with TLS/MTLS support
  • Web Terminal — Access your servers directly from your browser
  • Team Collaboration — Full RBAC for your entire organization
  • Real-time Monitoring — Track server health and active sessions
  • Audit Logs — Complete visibility into who accessed what and when
  • External Auth — Keycloak integration for OIDC/SSO authentication

Two Editions, One Mission​

We believe secure privileged access management should be accessible to everyone:

EditionFor
CommunityIndividual developers and small teams who need core PAM features with TLS security
EnterpriseOrganizations requiring OIDC/SSO (Keycloak), MTLS, and multi-tenancy support
